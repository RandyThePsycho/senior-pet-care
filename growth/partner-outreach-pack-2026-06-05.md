# Partner Outreach Pack - 2026-06-05

## Goal

Use the partner kit to get qualified senior-pet families into the Feature A
loop without spam, hidden promotion, or medical claims.

Primary URL:

`https://pawcheckin.com/partners/senior-pet-check-in-kit?utm_source=partner_outreach&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=senior_pet_checkin_kit&utm_content=general`

## Best-Fit Partner Types

### Priority 1

- senior dog rescues
- senior cat rescues
- shelter alumni newsletters
- pet hospice newsletters
- mobile vet newsletters that share non-diagnostic prep resources

### Priority 2

- pet parent newsletter writers
- senior pet Facebook group admins
- local rescue volunteers who maintain resource pages
- dog/cat aging-content creators

### Defer

- ecommerce stores
- affiliate publishers
- generic pet product influencers
- emergency/medical diagnosis communities

## Outreach Rules

- Do not imply veterinary advice.
- Do not ask for user data.
- Do not ask for a paid placement yet.
- Offer a free resource they can inspect first.
- Ask whether it would be useful, not whether they can "promote" us.
- Use one link only.

## Email / Contact Form Draft

Free senior pet check-in resource for older dog/cat families

Body:

Hi,

I’m building PawCheckin, a free senior pet check-in resource for families caring

7-day follow-up journal.
