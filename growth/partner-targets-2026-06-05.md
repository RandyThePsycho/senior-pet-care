# Partner Target List - 2026-06-05

## Goal And Metric

Goal:
Get PawCheckin in front of senior-pet families through trusted rescue,
sanctuary, hospice, and education partners.

Primary metric:
Partner kit visits and downstream 7-day reassessment rate, not raw social
impressions.

Partner kit base URL:

`https://pawcheckin.com/partners/senior-pet-check-in-kit`

URL check:
All target URLs in this file returned HTTP `200` on 2026-06-05.

## Risk Check

- Do not imply diagnosis, prognosis, treatment, emergency triage, or veterinary
  authority.
- Do not ask partners to hand over user data.
- Do not pitch paid placement yet.
- Use one link only.
- For hospice/end-of-life adjacent partners, lead with vet-conversation notes
  and observation organization, not product language or growth language.
- Do not submit contact forms or DMs until the sender identity/account is
  confirmed by the user.

## First Outreach Message Variant

Use this for Batch 1 contact forms or email:

Subject:
Free senior pet check-in resource for older dog/cat families

Body:

Hi,

I am building PawCheckin, a free senior pet check-in resource for families
caring for older dogs and cats.

It includes senior dog/cat checklists, vet-visit notes, and a
quality-of-life calculator that turns observations into a printable report and
7-day follow-up journal.

It is not a diagnosis or veterinary advice. The goal is simply to help families
organize what they are noticing before speaking with a licensed veterinarian.

Here is the partner kit if you want to review it:

`https://pawcheckin.com/partners/senior-pet-check-in-kit?utm_source=partner_outreach&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=senior_pet_checkin_kit&utm_content=TARGET_SLUG`

If this feels useful for your community, I would be grateful if you shared it as
a free resource. If not, no pressure at all.

Thank you,
Randy
