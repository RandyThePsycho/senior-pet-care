# Growth Channel Matrix

This launch plan keeps Senior Pet Care positioned as an observation and vet-conversation tool, not a diagnosis product.

## What We Can Use From The Marketing Skill Screenshots

- **SEO Optimization:** Use search intent first. Prioritize pages around senior dog/cat quality-of-life tracking, vet question preparation, mobility changes, appetite changes, night confusion, incontinence, and gentle end-of-life planning.
- **Content-to-Pipeline:** Turn one core idea into many formats: SEO FAQ, Reddit/Quora answer, Pinterest checklist pin, X/Threads post, short video script, and newsletter note.
- **Social Media Management:** Cover mainstream channels, but post natively and manually at first. Do not automate mass posting or repeat the same link everywhere.
- **Email Sequence:** Keep the Feature A loop central: report delivery, 7-day reassessment reminder, journal nudge, and gentle topic-based follow-up.
- **Analytics & Attribution:** Every public campaign link should use UTM parameters so email captures and need submissions can be tied back to source.

Defer paid UGC ads, broad cold email, and heavy automation until we know which organic channels produce email captures and 7-day reassessments.

## Priority Order

| Priority | Channel | Goal | First CTA | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Google Search / Bing | High-intent discovery | Calculator / checklist | Slow ramp |
| 2 | Reddit | Customer research + careful answers | Ask permission before linking | High spam sensitivity |
| 3 | Quora | Durable answer traffic | One helpful link when relevant | Medium spam sensitivity |
| 4 | Pinterest | Printable/report/checklist discovery | Calculator or checklist | Needs visual assets |
| 5 | YouTube Shorts | Awareness + trust | Link in description/profile | Needs simple video workflow |
| 6 | TikTok | Awareness + empathy-led education | Link in bio | Health claims risk |
| 7 | Instagram Reels | Caregiver-friendly education | Link in bio/story | Lower direct intent |
| 8 | Facebook Groups | Community learning | Usually no link unless allowed | Group rules vary |
| 9 | X / Threads | Build-in-public + short tips | Occasional link | Low conversion alone |
| 10 | LinkedIn | Founder credibility + partnerships | Website/profile link | Less direct pet-parent intent |
| 11 | Rescue / hospice / mobile vet partners | Trust-based referrals | Direct resource link | Requires outreach |
| 12 | Newsletter swaps | Relevant audience borrowing | Calculator / checklist | Needs partner fit |

## What Not To Do

- Do not mass-post the same text and link across communities.
- Do not imply diagnosis, prognosis, treatment, emergency triage, or vet replacement.
- Do not promote product recommendations in end-of-life contexts.
- Do not hide affiliation when sharing the site in communities.
- Do not judge channels by page views alone. Track email capture, report opens, journal opens, and 7-day reassessment.
